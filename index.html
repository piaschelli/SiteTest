Our Story

Founded in 2011 with a simple but relentless vision.

We founded Studio Linear in 2011 with a simple but relentless vision: to create the kind of bold, boundary-pushing design we weren't seeing anywhere else.

What started as a solo operation has grown into an award-winning, female-owned creative agency — a tight-knit team of strategists, designers, developers, and writers who genuinely love what they do.

We've built our reputation by taking on the kind of work that demands real craft: regulated industries, culture-driven brands, CPG packaging that stops people dead in the aisle. We're not here to churn out cookie-cutter work — we're here to make things that matter.

Over the past decade we've partnered with brands ranging from scrappy startups to household names, helping each one find a visual identity as distinct as what they stand for.
